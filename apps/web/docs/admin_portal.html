🌾 Farmer is the only role with a multi-step farm + crop setup wizard. All other roles use a compact 3-step form (personal info → role details → review).
Role Quick Reference
🌾 Farmer
Access: Mobile app + USSD. Farm creation is web-only (done by admin here). After creation: assign lender + extension officer. Can request loans, diagnose crops, sell produce.
🏦 Lender Rep
Reviews loan applications. Needs: institution name, CBK licence, M-Pesa paybill, contact person.
📦 Supplier
Lists products in marketplace. Needs: business reg no., product categories, delivery radius, county.
🩺 Vet / Ext. Officer
Reviews AI diagnoses, answers community Q&A. Needs: KVB licence or ministry cert, specialisation.
🏛 Govt Officer
Manages subsidy applications, farm registrations. Needs: ministry, county, staff ID.
🤝 Buyer
Browses produce listings, places orders. Needs: company, trade type, counties of interest.
👷 Farm Worker
Assigned to specific farm. Role: manager / field_worker / harvester / sprayer / driver. Created as user in auth_db.
